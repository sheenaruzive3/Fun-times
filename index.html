Welcome, Tinokugamuchirai, Siyakwamukela

Welcome to Jane Rivulet Academy — a home of learning, love, and purpose.


Nestled on a peaceful peri-urban farm along Harmony Road in Harben Park, Gweru, our school proudly serves the surrounding farm community with a deep sense of responsibility and compassion. At Jane Rivulet Academy, we believe every child deserves access to quality education, and we are committed to creating an environment where all learners can grow, thrive, and discover their God-given potential.


Our mission is to nurture children academically, socially, and spiritually—empowering them with the knowledge, skills, and character needed for excellence in both school and life. We strive to build a happy, safe, and supportive space where learners develop a genuine love for learning, one that will guide and strengthen them throughout their lives. Here, we encourage every child to “drink from the streams of knowledge,” growing into confident future leaders and responsible, God-fearing citizens of tomorrow.


Our Christian values are woven into our daily operations, guiding everything we do with integrity, care, and purpose. If you are seeking a school that places your child’s best interests at heart, then Jane Rivulet Academy is your home.


We warmly welcome you to our family.
